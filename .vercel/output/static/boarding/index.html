Boarding

Our boarding service offers comfortable accommodation, experienced care and health monitoring, ensuring your guinea pigs are well looked after while you are away. In addition, all proceeds from boarding go toward funding the rescue and allow us to continue to help guinea pigs in need.

What we Offer:

  • Spacious Cages: Your guinea pigs will enjoy large, secure enclosures equipped with fleece or vetbed liners for their comfort.
  • Daily Care: We provide fresh water, plenty of hay, high-quality nuggets, and a variety of fresh veggies daily to keep your piggies happy and healthy.
  • Health Checks: We conduct weekly weight checks and health inspections to monitor their well-being, and we will increase the frequency if we have any concerns.
  • Outdoor & Indoor Play Areas: On nice days, your guinea pigs will have access to large outdoor runs where they can graze on fresh grass. During poor weather, we offer indoor play pens for a fun, safe environment.

Additional Services:

  • Medication: We can administer medication to guinea pigs that require it, but please discuss this with us in advance to ensure we can meet your pet's needs.
  • Veterinary Care: If your guinea pig falls ill during their stay, we’ll ensure they receive prompt attention from our experienced vet.

Pricing:

  • £13 per night for a pair of guinea pigs (+ £5 per night per additional piggy).
  • We are happy to discuss discounted rates for large groups of guinea pigs and extended stays.

Book in Advance

Please note that we get busy during peak periods, especially school holidays. Book early to secure a space for your guinea pigs and ensure they have a comfortable place to stay while you’re away!
